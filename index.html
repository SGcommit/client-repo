D&D 5e (2014) Encounter Generator

How does it work?

Our encounter generator takes parameters, such as party size and environment the fight is taking place in. This info is inputted by YOU the user, and is handed off to our AI model, who will think really hard to come up with enemies your party can fight. After the AI comes up with what enemies your party can fight, it'll query another D&D API to grab information about the enemies, that way you're not accidentally sending AI homebrew at your party.

Is it free?

Yes and no. Our generator will allow you to make 20 encounters on a single account, then you will be out of free encounters. To get more encounters, contact us at 5eGenerator@gmail.com to set up a payment plan for your account.
